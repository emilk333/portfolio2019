Futurenow

Futurenow is a financial service concept designed as part of my master thesis project in collaboration with Danske Bank Group’s Customer Engagement department.

The challenge was to design a financial service which could potentially ease negative feelings associated with unexpected expenses for young adults, by focusing on an improved service user experience and functionality provided by design thinking.

The project was developed with two other fantastic individuals from my univeristy. Scroll down to see a brief overview of the design process.

1

Understanding the problem area

In order to acquire an understanding of the different users and stakeholders, interviews were conducted on back employees and the target user group. The same user group where subsequently invited to a co-creative workshop to explore ideas and further strengthening their life and the needs associated

2

User data analysis

The insights gained from the empirical work were analysed via affinity diagramming, which resulted in eco-system maps and design principles. More so, the data also allowed us to develop user journeys, ultimately supporting us in ideating on a final service concept.

3

Testing the prototype

Based on the previous insights a concept was sketched and prototyped in low fidelity. The prototype was created in Adobe XD and was fully functional on a mobile device. Subsequently we asked potential users to evaluate the prototype, by allowing them to complete a series of tasks on prototype.

Final concept

The final concept is a service concept, which is placed within the Mobile Bank. With the flick of switch the user can switch to a future view which allows them to see their bank accounts balance a week, month and a year ahead.

In addition, the user can see what attributes to said account balance, which allows the user to not only be reminded of unforseen expenses, but also gain an understanding of what constitutes their economy.

Lastly, the concept will also generate insights, which informs the user on various economic subject relevant to their situation.

By allowing users to gain insights onto their economic situation and the different components consituting it, the service concept should hopefully ease the negative feelings associated with unexpeced expenses through foresight.
