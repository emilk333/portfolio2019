Problem defined

who was involved

1

Understanding the problem area

In order to acquire an understanding of the different users and stakeholders, interviews were conducted on back employees and the target user group. The same user group where subsequently invited to a co-creative workshop to explore ideas and further strengthening their life and the needs associated

2

User data analysis

The insights gained from the empirical work were analysed via affinity diagramming, which resulted in eco-system maps and design principles. More so, the data also allowed us to develop user journeys, ultimately supporting us in ideating on a final service concept.

3

Testing the prototype

Based on the previous insights a concept was sketched and prototyped in low fidelity. The prototype was created in Adobe XD and was fully functional on a mobile device. Subsequently we asked potential users to evaluate the prototype, by allowing them to complete a series of tasks on prototype.
